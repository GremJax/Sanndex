# Sanndex
A public information repository that assigns a reputation score to internet news providers on accountability, trust, and sensationalism.

## Scoring system:
The Sanndex rating system is universal and unambiguous, with peer review and report referencing to ensure no ratings are biased, influenced, or misrepresentative beyond reasonable doubt. The metric consists of 6 scoring fields rated 1 to 100, with a rubric style grouping system. The total score is the average across all scores.

### Accuracy
Is your information factual? Does your information contain errors or unmarked speculation?

- 90-100: Information is fully accurate. All speculation is sufficiently labeled. 

### Transparency
How is your content created? Are you consistent with dates, authorship, and funding?

- 90-100: All contributers are properly creditted. All content has publish dates and appropriate method description. Funding contributions are publicly stated or otherwise easily accessible.

### Integrity
Do you correct your mistakes? Do you hold yourself to a high standard of accountability?

- 90-100: All mistakes are corrected swiftly and publicly. 

### Manipulation
Do you tell the truth to your audience? Is your information misconstrued or sensationalist?

- 90-100: Information is fully neutral. No information is withheld or cherry picked. No political bias is present towards information-restrictive groups.

### Authenticity
Are you who you say you are? Is your content AI generated and presented as real?

- 90-100: All non-obvious use of artificial intelligence is publicly creditted and watermarked. No attempts are made to conceal the production methods or funding avenues.

### Credibility
Where does your information come from? Do you cite sources, experts, and true accounts?

- 90-100: All sources are properly cited. All involved surveys are unbiased and comprehensive. All experts are credible and relevant.
